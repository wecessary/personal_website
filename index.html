About

Hello! My name is Wesley. I make things in Python and am an economist.

I have three years of experience as an economic consultant, where I have used data analysis and econometrics to guide public policy for public sector clients such as HM Treasury, UK Space Agency, and the European Commission.

Before then I was a Visiting Lecturer at City University teaching econometrics and data analysis. I also taught microeconomics at the London School of Economics, for which I earned the Highly Commended Class Teacher prize.

I left my previous role to explore things that I can do with coding beyond data analysis. This website is one of the things that I've made. Check out some of the other things that I've made so far below.
